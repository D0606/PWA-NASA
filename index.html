About this project


This project uses various NASA Open APIs to do a variety of interesting activities. You can navigate between these activities by selecting an option from the navbar at the top of the screen. Each page will have an expanded description of the API and features, along with instructions on how to use it. Please consider completing my feedback form below!


- APOD: The NASA Astronomical Picture of the Day (APOD) is described as one of the most popular sites of all federal agencies in the United States. Media can be video or photograph and is displayed with its description and title!

- NEO: The Near Earth Object Web Service (NeoWs) provides asteroid information based on their closest approach date. Enter a date and see which asteroids are visiting!

- Landsat: Landsat 8 is an 8-year-old near-polar orbit imaging satellite. Select a date and zoom level and see period specific land satellite imagery from your GPS location!

- Gallery: Several large resolution pictures of incredible sights and scenes, provided by NASA. The images are displayed in an art-directed format for viewing on several devices!

- Settings: If you want a change of colour, visit here to cycle to the night side or vice versa!
